Detailed Description

template<>
struct llvm::PointerLikeTypeTraits< mlir::Identifier >

The pointer inside of an identifier comes from a StringMap, so its alignment is always at least 4 and probably 8 (on 64-bit machines).

Allow LLVM to steal the low bits.
